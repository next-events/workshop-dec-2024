We are pleased to announce the NExT Singapore Workshop 2024, which will take place from December 2 to 6, 2024. The theme of this year’s workshop is “Responsible AI and LLMs”, featuring a series of focused activities:

    • 2-3 Dec: Workshop on AI Policy, led by Prof. Simon Chesterman from NUS Law Faculty.
    • 4-5 Dec: NExT Workshop focusing on "Responsible AI and LLMs".
    • 6 Dec: Discussion on AI trends and future planning for NExT Workshops.

The AI Policy Workshop will be held at the NUS campus (exact location to be confirmed), while the activities from December 4 to 6 will take place at Level 01-03 Seminar Room, Innovation 4.0, NUS, Singapore 117602.

This workshop is designed to bring together NExT members and collaborators to explore the advancements, applications, and future pathways of Responsible AI and LLMs. Participation is by invitation only, with registration limited to invitees.

More details will be shared soon—stay tuned! We look forward to seeing you in Singapore.
